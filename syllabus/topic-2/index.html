2.1 Equations of straight lines, parallel and perpendicular

Forms of a line

y = mx + c
ax + by + d = 0
y-y_1=m(x-x_1)

Gradient between two points

m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}
  • Parallel lines have equal gradients.
  • Perpendicular lines have gradients whose product is -1.
