4.4 Probability Basics

P(A)=\frac{n(A)}{n(U)}

Complement

P(A')=1-P(A)

4.5 Combined Events

P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(A\cap B)

Mutually exclusive

P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B)

4.6 Conditional Probability

P(A\mid B)=\frac{P(A\cap B)}{P(B)}

4.7 Expected Value

E(X)=\sum x_iP(X=x_i)
