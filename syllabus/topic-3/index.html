Topic 3: Geometry and Trigonometry

Prior Learning (Assumed)

Area

A_{\text{triangle}}=\frac{1}{2}bh
A_{\text{parallelogram}}=bh
A_{\text{trapezoid}}=\frac{1}{2}(a+b)h
A_{\text{circle}}=\pi r^2

Volume

V_{\text{cuboid}}=lwh
V_{\text{cylinder}}=\pi r^2h
V_{\text{prism}}=Ah

3.1 Distance in Space

Distance between two points

d=\sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2}

3.2 Non-right-angled Trigonometry

Sine rule

\frac{a}{\sin A}=\frac{b}{\sin B}=\frac{c}{\sin C}

Cosine rule

c^2=a^2+b^2-2ab\cos C

Area of a triangle

A=\frac{1}{2}ab\sin C

3.3 Trigonometric Functions

  • \sin x, \cos x, \tan x
  • Periodicity and graphs

3.4 Radian Measure

Arc length

l=r\theta

Sector area

A=\frac{1}{2}r^2\theta

3.5 Trigonometric Identities

\tan\theta=\frac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta}

3.6 Pythagorean & Double-Angle Identities

\sin^2\theta+\cos^2\theta=1
\sin2\theta=2\sin\theta\cos\theta
\cos2\theta=\cos^2\theta-\sin^2\theta
